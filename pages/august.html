HELLO, MONTIGNY!

Oh, how happy we are to be together at last! I mean, I know it was only a week, but things just feel so much better when we're together. I arrived at CDG in the early morning and waited a quick 8 hours for Josh to be available to pick me up to take me to our new home - Montigny-sur-Loing. I was feeling a bit peckish after that wait, so we stopped in Fontainebleau for a bite to eat. We stopped at the first gorgeous little outside cafe we saw. Exhausted we ordered what was almost certianly a frozen pizza and just enjoyed the view.

Of course we had to stop for Amorino.

12 August 2021

WE LOVE YOU, CHICAGO

After a couple all-nighters, endless hours of page refreshing, and lots of prayers, we finally got a visa appointment in Chicago. What this basically means is that we arrive at 6am, go to our visa appointments until lunch, and then have the day to explore the city. It was a beautiful, crisp day - exactly the kind of summer weather you could hope for in this beautiful state. The appointment was rather uneventul - with the exception of a Frenchman who threw his paperwork to the ground because he didn't want to give up his passport to get the visa.

A lovely 24hrs in the city!
